WORK PROCESS

For us, it all starts here, we invest plenty of time to understand our customers' needs and desired business outcomes. We play back our understanding of the project and get an agreement on the scope of works project costs. We don’t like any surprises and our clients appreciate the transparency.
Once we understand the project requirements, we set about putting the right team together. We take time to carefully select the right people for the job based on their skill-sets and ability to work together. We only select the best people for our clients
We spend a fair bit of time on selecting which technologies and methodology will be used. We break down larger apps into Microservices to reduce any time wastage and optimise our team delivery. Collectively we plan our sprints, engage key stake-holders and get upfront agreement on each sprint deliverable.
Our preference is for the Agile Iterative delivery, allowing us to optimise code early on and release value up front. We have clear checkpoints in the development cycles that help capture and correct any code/design errors early on and address in subsequent iterations.
Our team perform regular deep dives to inspect the quality of code. We look for bugs, code style policy violations, security vulnerabilities, code complexity, memory leeks and such. In addition, the QA team uses a combination of Automated and Manual testing to ensure the code is performing to expectations.
We prefer to use Jira and Confluence however if in-person stand-ups are your preference, we are the right people for the job. We are committed to delivering the best outcomes for our clients, so regular catchups and demos are our priority. We will give you plenty of time to raise any concerns and will work tirelessly to address the same.
