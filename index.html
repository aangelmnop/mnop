How we do things

How we do things

CX driven outcomes

We strongly believe that the overall customer experience and usability deliver great business outcomes. We take time to understand the client's requirements and build our teams accordingly. We put customer experience first.

Communication

All too often projects fail or run overtime and budget due to poor communication. We guarantee frequent, transparent communication with our clients, with regular meetings and ensuring we stay on track.

Methodology Used

We prefer to use the Agile development approach giving our clients maximum ability to tweak requirements however we are no stranger to the more traditional Waterfall approach if the clear outcomes are pre-determined.

Building the right team

Once we have a full grasp of the clients' requirements we set about building the right team for the project. Different projects have different needs, we take our time to identify the best people for the job.

Code reviews

Code reviews is a must for us. We aim to deliver code that is easy to understand, follow and adapt as such, regular code reviews is our second nature. Our goal is to be at the best in the game so we aim to stay ahead of the curve.

Quality Assurance

Quality Assurance and Testing is the integral part of our development cycle and we utilise both ATDD and TDD methodology to ensure we deliver quality code.
